About Us

Delivering Ethiopia’s Finest to the World – Quality You Can Trust!

Nebil Trading is a dynamic and forward-thinking export company established in 2024, headquartered in Ethiopia. Specializing in the export of high-quality agricultural products, we are dedicated to delivering premium oil seeds, pulses, and spices to global markets. Our product portfolio includes Red Kidney Beans, Sesame Seeds, Soya Beans, Green Mung Beans, and a variety of other oil seeds, all sourced from the fertile lands of Ethiopia.

At Nebil Trading, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. By leveraging Ethiopia’s rich agricultural heritage and our expertise in international trade, we aim to bridge the gap between Ethiopian farmers and global consumers, ensuring that our products meet the highest standards of excellence.
