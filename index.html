About Us

Delivering Ethiopia’s Finest to the World – Quality You Can Trust!

Nebil Trading is a dynamic and forward-thinking export company established in 2024, headquartered in Ethiopia. Specializing in the export of high-quality agricultural products, we are dedicated to delivering premium oil seeds, pulses, and spices to global markets.
